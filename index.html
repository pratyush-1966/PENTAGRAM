Sankhyotsav

Sankhyotsav, the annual mathematical fest organized by the esteemed PENTAGRAM Club, is a celebration of the fascinating world of mathematics. This year, the fest promises to be grander than ever, with a series of engaging events scheduled from July 9th to 12th. The highlight of Sankhyotsav is the collaboration with various clubs, bringing a diverse range of activities that blend mathematics with different disciplines. These collaborative events aim to challenge and inspire participants, fostering a deeper appreciation for the beauty and application of mathematics. Students and enthusiasts from all backgrounds are welcome to join this exciting journey, making Sankhyotsav a truly inclusive and enriching experience.
